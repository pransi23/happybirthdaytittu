💌 My Love Letter to You

My dearest Simran (Tittu),

Meri Pyaari Simran (Tittu), Janamdin ki lakh-lakh badhaiyaan ❤️ Aaj ka din mere liye sirf ek special date nahi hai, balki us pal ka celebration hai jab meri zindagi mein aap aaye. Aapka hona meri duniya ka sabse khoobsurat hissa hai. Aapke bina zindagi adhoori si lagti hai… jaise koi geet ho lekin sur na ho, jaise chand ho lekin roshni na ho. Tittu, aapki muskaan meri subah hai, aapki awaaz mera sukoon hai, aur aapka pyaar meri har khushi ka raaz hai. Jab bhi main udaas hota hoon, bas aapka naam soch leta hoon... aur sab kuch theek lagne lagta hai. Itna asar hai aapka mujh par. Main hamesha chahta hoon ki aap khush rahen, muskurate rahen, aur har wo cheez paayein jo aap deserve karti hain. Aap sirf meri mohabbat nahi ho, meri respect ho, meri har dua ka jawab ho, aur meri har kal ka sapna bhi. Main har janam mein yahi chahta hoon — ki aap meri zindagi mein ho, meri partner ho, meri Tittu ho. Aapke saath jeevan ka har pal ek nayi kahani likhta hai, jisme sirf pyaar, vishwas, aur ek gehra bandhan hota hai. Aaj ke din main sirf itna kehna chahta hoon: Main aapse bepanah pyaar karta hoon, aur yeh pyaar kabhi kam nahi hoga. Aap meri sabse khoobsurat adaat ban chuki hain, aur main aapko apni har saans mein mehsoos karta hoon. Janamdin mubarak ho, meri jaan. Mera pyaar, meri izzat, meri Tittu… hamesha aapke saath hoon, har kadam par. 💖 Hamesha aapka, Pranav

Happy birthday, my jaan.

Forever yours,
pranav
